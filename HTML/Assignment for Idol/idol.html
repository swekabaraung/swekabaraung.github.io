David Lai is a singer-songwriter from Myanmar, who following his dream sequentially and continuously deals. His voice,his attitude ad his will would describe David as a man. His personality is so magnetic with god blessings.

After departing his native land in 2021 due to the military coup,he is lighting up the world armed with his guitar and stellar vocal from his new base in India. Equally strong performing in his native Burmese or in English, David has amassed a huge base of fans from across the world, with audiences of over 500,000 on Facebook, 100,000 on YouTube and 18,000 on Instagram following every step of David’s remarkable story. Often performing in beautiful natural surroundings, over 24,000,000 people have tuned in to David’s passionate, thought-provoking performances on YouTube to date. David will be working with Ostereo throughout 2022 to keep honing his craft and to reach more people across the world than ever before, with highly anticipated original material to edge over the horizon toward the end of the year.
